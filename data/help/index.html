Welcome to Zyzzyva!

Zyzzyva is a program to help you study words and practice your anagramming skills. It allows you to search for words, anagrams, and definitions, as well as give yourself quizzes. Zyzzyva is particularly valuable for learning words for word games. Zyzzyva also has a full screen Word Judge mode, which can be useful for determining the validity of words while playing word games.

Zyzzyva is Free Software, available under the GNU General Public License. The latest version can always be found at Zyzzyva.net.

Further Help

If you need help beyond what is available in the documentation, try one of these three options, in this order:

  • Mailing list: Send your question or suggestion to the Zyzzyva Users mailing list. There are many users on that list who generally respond quickly and helpfully to most questions.
  • Issue tracker: Enter a bug report or feature request into the Zyzzyva issue tracker. Please search for existing issues to make sure your issue is not already in the tracker.
  • Personal email: Send me (Michael Thelen) a personal email, at mthelen@gmail.com. However, I get a lot of email and will probably take a while to respond, if I am able to respond at all.
