Welcome to Zyzzyva V !

Zyzzyva V is a program to help you study words and practice your anagramming skills. It allows you to search for words, anagrams, and definitions, as well as give yourself quizzes. Zyzzyva V is particularly valuable for learning words for word games. Zyzzyva V also has a full screen Word Judge mode, which can be useful for determining the validity of words while playing word games.

Zyzzyva V is Free Software, available under the GNU General Public License. The latest version can always be found at Collins.
